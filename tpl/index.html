Amazon Web Services Report Visualizer

This project has been discontinued
due to lack of public interest and because Amazon are changing their format every few months.
You can download the sources and use them as you need for free.

What is this?

This simple web tool lets you see your AWS Usage Reports by visualizing them in charts.

Why do I need this?

Analyzing the graphics lets you see the trends, and also transforms the raw numbers of your AWS Account Activity into something more beautiful and easy to analyze by humans.

How can I use it?

Here are the steps:

  1. Log in to your Amazon Web Services account and then navigate to "Usage Reports". There you can download your Usage reports for different services.
  2. Select "Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud" or "Amazon Simple Storage Service" (S3) from the Service menu.
  3. There are certain filter criterias. You can leave the defaults or alter for you needs.
  4. Click "Download report (CSV)" and save the file on your computer.
  5. In the form below, on this web page, upload the CSV file and review the generated graphics by Google Charts.
